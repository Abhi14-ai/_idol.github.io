Vinayaka Chaturthi

Vinayaka Chaturthi June 2021: Date, tithi and other significant details

Vinayaka Chaturthi June 2021: As per the Hindu calendar, each lunar month has two Chaturthi Tithis. As per Hindu scriptures, it is believed that Chaturthi Tithi(s) belongs to Lord Ganesha. The Chaturthi after Amavasya or new moon during Shukla Paksha is known as Vinayaka Chaturthi. Vinayaka Chaturthi commemorates the monthly birthday of Lord Ganesha. On this day, devotes of Lord Ganesha observe a fast on the Chaturthi Tithi of Shukla Paksha. Vinayaka Chaturthi is also known as Varad Vinayaka Chaturthi.
